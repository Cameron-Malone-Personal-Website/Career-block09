The Great Outdoors

Being outdoors is the greatest gift given to the world. As a frequent backpacker, nothing cures the body, mind, and soul like being outdoors. On the hardest trecks, the voice telling you this is to hard is loud. But after every outing, that voice becomes a little more quiet. Until one day there is no voice of doubt and just the pleasantries of the great outdoors.
